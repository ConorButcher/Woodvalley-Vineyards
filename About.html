ABOUT US

Woodvalley Vineyards

Welcome to the enchanting world of Woodvalley Vineyards! Nestled amidst the picturesque landscapes of Billericay, our family-owned vineyard is a testament to our passion for crafting exceptional wines. With a rich history spanning generations, we take immense pride in cultivating grapes of the finest quality and transforming them into exquisite wines that reflect the unique character of our terroir.

At Woodvalley Vineyards, we combine traditional winemaking techniques with a modern approach to create wines that captivate the senses. Our vineyard benefits from the region's fertile soil, abundant sunlight, and a temperate climate, resulting in grapes that possess remarkable flavors and complexity. From the crisp and refreshing whites to the elegant and robust reds, each bottle showcases our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Beyond our commitment to producing outstanding wines, we invite you to immerse yourself in the beauty of our vineyard. Whether you're a wine enthusiast seeking an unforgettable tasting experience or a nature lover yearning for a tranquil escape, our vineyard offers a haven where you can unwind and reconnect with the land. Join us for a tour, indulge in a wine tasting, or simply bask in the serenity of the surrounding vineyard as you savor the fruits of our labor. Billericay Vineyard is more than just a winery—it's an invitation to embark on a sensory journey and create lasting memories.

Our History

Inception and Vineyard Establishment

2022

The first milestone in the journey of a vineyard begins with the conception of the idea and the meticulous planning that follows. It involves selecting a suitable location, analyzing soil and climate conditions, and acquiring the necessary land for planting vineyards. This phase also includes the essential steps of sourcing quality grapevines, planting them, and establishing the infrastructure required for vineyard management.

Winemaking and Aging

2023

After the grapes are harvested, the focus shifts to winemaking. This milestone encompasses the intricate process of crushing the grapes, fermenting the juice, and carefully monitoring the temperature, yeast activity, and maceration period. Once fermentation is complete, the winemaker makes crucial decisions regarding blending, aging, and oak barrel selection. The wines are then left to age in a controlled environment, allowing them to develop their distinct flavors, aromas, and complexities.

Vine Growth and Harvest

2022

Once the vineyard is established, the next milestone revolves around the growth and nurturing of the grapevines. This phase requires dedicated care, including regular pruning, trellising, pest management, and irrigation, to ensure optimal vine health and grape development. Over the span of several years, the vines will mature and bear their first fruit. The milestone culminates with the eagerly anticipated harvest season, where the grapes are carefully handpicked or mechanically harvested.

First Bottle Release and Market Debut

2024

The final milestone marks the culmination of years of hard work, dedication, and patience—the release of the vineyard's first bottle of wine. This significant moment signifies the winery's official entry into the market and the opportunity to share their passion and craftsmanship with wine enthusiasts. It involves labeling and packaging the bottles, organizing launch events, and introducing the wines to local wine shops, restaurants, and consumers. The release of the first bottle represents a remarkable achievement and serves as a foundation for future growth, establishing the vineyard's reputation and paving the way for subsequent vintages.
